Shipping Policy

Transparent shipping terms for a seamless delivery experience.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days after payment confirmation.

Custom or personalized products may require longer processing times; you will be notified accordingly.

Delivery Timeframes

  • Standard delivery within India usually takes 5-7 business days from shipment.
  • International shipping times vary depending on the destination and customs processing.
  • Delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances, including weather or carrier issues.

Shipping Charges

Shipping fees are calculated based on weight, dimensions, and destination, and are displayed at checkout.

Free shipping offers may apply based on promotional terms, which will be clearly indicated.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor delivery status.

If you do not receive a tracking number within 3 days of order confirmation, please contact support.

Damages and Lost Shipments

  • Please inspect your package upon receipt and report any damages or missing items within 48 hours.
  • We are not responsible for delays or losses caused by the shipping carrier beyond our control.
  • In case of damage or loss, contact us immediately for resolution assistance.
