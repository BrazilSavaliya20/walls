Cancellation & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Overview

We strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations, returns, and refunds for products purchased from our store.

Order Cancellation

Before Shipment

  • Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing the order
  • No cancellation fee will be charged for orders cancelled within this timeframe
  • Full refund will be processed to the original payment method
  • Cancellation requests must be made via email or phone (contact details below)

After Shipment

  • Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled
  • You may return the product upon delivery following our return policy

Quick Tip

To cancel your order quickly, have your order number ready and contact us immediately after placing the order.

Return Policy

Eligibility Criteria

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be in original condition with all tags and packaging intact
  • Items should be unworn, unused, and unwashed
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase is required

Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized or personalized products
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Perishable goods
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear and tear

Important Notice

Returns without original packaging or in damaged condition may be rejected or subject to a restocking fee of up to 25% of the item value.

Refund Process

Refund Timeline

  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item
  • Credit Card: 5-10 business days from processing date
  • Debit Card: 3-7 business days from processing date
  • Net Banking: 3-7 business days from processing date
  • UPI/Wallet: 1-3 business days from processing date

Refund Method

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase. In cases where the original payment method is unavailable, we may issue store credit or process the refund through an alternative method after verification.

Refund Protection

All refunds are processed securely through our payment gateway. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been initiated.

Return Shipping

Return Shipping Costs

  • Defective/Wrong Items: Free return shipping (we provide prepaid return label)
  • Change of Mind: Customer bears return shipping costs
  • Size/Fit Issues: Customer bears return shipping costs (unless it's our sizing error)

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Receive return authorization and instructions
  3. Package the item securely with all original accessories
  4. Ship using the provided return label or your chosen courier
  5. Track the return shipment
  6. Receive refund confirmation once processed

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide clear photos of the damaged/defective item
  • We will arrange for immediate replacement or full refund
  • No return shipping charges will be applied

Photo Requirements

Please provide clear, well-lit photos showing the defect or damage. This helps us process your claim quickly and improve our quality control.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in the following situations:

  • Items returned without original packaging
  • Items with minor damage not reported within 48 hours
  • Items returned after the 7-day return window (at management discretion)
  • Restocking fee for large or bulky items

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, we offer exchanges for size or color variants subject to availability. The exchange process follows the same timeline as returns.

What if I lose my receipt?

We can process returns using your order number, email confirmation, or bank transaction details as proof of purchase.

Can I return sale or discounted items?

Yes, sale items can be returned within the same 7-day window and following the same conditions as regular-priced items.

Need Help?
